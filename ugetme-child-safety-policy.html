Child Safety and Protection Policy

Zero‑tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation (CSAE).

ugetme is a social matching application committed to protecting children and young people. We maintain a zero‑tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any form of child sexual exploitation (CSAE). We remove such content and accounts immediately and report to appropriate authorities where required.

Effective date 2025-11-05

Scope and definitions

  • CSAM/CSAE: Any content or behavior that depicts, promotes, solicits, or facilitates sexual abuse or exploitation of a minor (a person under 18).
  • Zero tolerance: No depiction, sharing, solicitation, grooming, or attempts to obtain sexual content from or involving minors.

Prohibited content and conduct

  • Creation, possession, distribution, or promotion of CSAM.
  • Sexualized comments, grooming, or solicitation involving minors.
  • Impersonation of a minor for sexual purposes.
  • Links to external sites, storage, or messaging that facilitate CSAM.

Age and account requirements

  • ugetme is for users aged 18+ only.
  • Underage accounts are removed when identified. Attempts to bypass age restrictions may result in a permanent ban.

Reporting CSAM or safety concerns

Users can report concerns in‑app using the Report and Block options on profiles and chats. You can also email us directly.

  • In‑app: Use Report/Block on the relevant profile or chat.
  • Email: alhroobferas@gmail.com

We prioritize child safety reports and act swiftly on credible reports.

Moderation and enforcement

  • We review reports, remove violating content, and suspend or ban accounts involved.
  • We preserve relevant data and, where legally required or appropriate, report to competent authorities.
  • Severe or repeated violations lead to permanent removal.

Cooperation with authorities

We cooperate with law enforcement and child protection organizations consistent with applicable laws and due process.

Data handling

  • Evidence related to CSAM/CSAE reports may be retained to meet legal obligations and to aid enforcement.
  • We handle such data securely and only share it as required by law.

Safety education and deterrence

  • We clearly warn users that CSAM/CSAE is illegal and strictly prohibited on ugetme.
  • We reserve the right to deploy safety tooling to detect, prevent, and deter abusive content and behavior.

Contact

For urgent child‑safety concerns, contact us at alhroobferas@gmail.com. Only law‑enforcement requests should come from official addresses.

Updates

We may update this policy to reflect legal requirements or product changes. Continued use of ugetme after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
